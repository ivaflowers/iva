Nice to meet you.

Hey there — my name is Iva, pronounced Ee-va (think WALLE's little robot friend).

I am a pop culture and art nerd from Belgrade, currently based in Munich. After studies of art and long experience in Visual Merchandising and fashion, I discovered passion for UX/UI design as a perfect sum of all of my interests.

Some random facts about me: as a student, I was a reporter on a local TV station since it was the perfect way to attend all the cultural events I wanted to experience. Besides that career, I am also a retired detective. As a 7 y/o, together with my best friend, I solved a string of robberies in our building. Unfortunately, police didn’t take our theory about gang dealing juice boxes serious, so that crime stays officially unsolved up to this day.

In my free time, I like to read, write short stories and ski.
